What we do

How is smart class™
technology different?

QualHub is committed to simplifying the entire training process. By providing robust software solutions and expert guidance in quality assurance and compliance, we enable training providers to deliver impactful learning experiences, hassle-free.

Traditional Training

  • Scattered PDF files, hard to read on mobile. No structured learning.
  • 20+ manual signatures required. Missing one can void results.
  • Paper-based assessments, missing details can lead to recalls.
  • No quizzes, mock exams, or interactive tools.
  • Paper-based exams, risk of errors, tampering, and lost results.
  • 10-14 days for results due to manual processing.

smart class™ technology

  • Mobile app with high-quality video content, like Netflix for learning
  • Digital paperwork with automatic checks—no admin errors
  • Digital assessments, error-free and validated instantly
  • Quizzes, mock exams, and video lessons for better preparation
  • Digital exams, securely connected to the exam server, no delays
  • Results within 24 hours—fast and efficient.
